# Game Design Doc

# Lore
You are an unidentified smooth black monolith.
As you pulsate energy, the odd primates around you slowly awaken to intelligence.
Guide them towards building the civilization required to support your escape from this planet.

# Mechanics
This game combines addictive idle game mechanics with Anki flashcards.
Starting state:
- Monolith pulses 1/s, generating 1 energy passive income per second.
- Reviewing cards generates 10 energy per card, regardless of ease level.

# UI
The top of the screen shows a log of in game events.
The scenario is explained in this log, similar to a text based adventure.
Other HUD widgets include a box that shows the income rates.
The Anki flashcards take up the center of the screen.
